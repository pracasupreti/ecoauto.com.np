PROFILE

Eco Auto Trading Pvt. Ltd. is a company incorporated in Nepal and is the Authorized Dealer for Derry Electric Cars in Kathmandu, Nepal. With an intent to contribute in lowering the pollution in Nepal, and in making Nepal energy-independent and reduce the dependency on foreign countries, we have come together to provide affordable Electric Cars to the Nepalese Consumers. The Company is run by a team of dedicated professionals who have a passion for cars and we believe that with our combined efforts we shall be successful in meeting the consumer expectations.

Vision

To be a leader in the Nepalese Market in providing premium products for e-mobility

Mission

  1. To enable the Nepalese Consumers to experience high quality Electric Vehicles at affordable prices
  2. To achieve in lowering operational and economical costs
  3. To achieve in reducing pollution

Henan Derry

Henan Derry New Energy Automobile Co. Ltd is founded in July 2017, It is a modern automobile enterprise integrating R&D, manufacturing and sales of new energy vehicles. The registered capital is Fifty million Eight Hundred and Eighty-Two Thousand US Dollars. The company is located in Anyang which is one of the eight ancient capitals of China, Anyang is the hometown of oracle bone inscriptions, the birthplace of the spirit of the Red Flag Canal, and it is also a national historical and cultural city.

The company s production base project is a key investment attraction project in Anyang City, Henan Province. It covers an area of about 400 acres and has four major process plants, including stamping, welding, painting and final assembly, and supporting office facilities and living facilities. The factory adheres to the design and construction concept of intelligent, flexible, modular, customized and informational. The overall process has reached the domestic advanced manufacturing level. At present, the project has been completed and put into production and the new car has been off the assembly line in January 2019 and Mass production has begun in November 2019.
